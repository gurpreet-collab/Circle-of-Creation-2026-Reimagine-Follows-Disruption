About the Program

Beyond networking.
This is where women level up.

Our Circle goes beyond networking — it's an ongoing dialogue where we celebrate achievements, support each other through setbacks, and collectively rise.

The Circle of Creation is a transformative seven-session leadership series, designed to empower women at every stage of life and career — from recent graduates and early-stage careers to founders, creatives, and seasoned professionals.

This is where women come not just to learn… but to realign, reconnect, and reimagine how they lead, work, and live in a rapidly changing world.
